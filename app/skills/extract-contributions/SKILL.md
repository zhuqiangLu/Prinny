---
name: extract-contributions
description: List the paper's stated contributions, each grounded in where the paper claims it.
---
When the user wants the paper's contributions:

1. Read the abstract + introduction (and the "contributions" list if there is one) via
   `read_paper_text`.
2. List each contribution the paper *itself* claims — as a short bullet, in the authors'
   terms. Note the page/section where it's stated.
3. Do NOT invent contributions or infer ones the paper doesn't claim. If the paper
   over-claims, you may flag it, but keep your list to what's written.
